Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur

Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT), Tumkur is a private college that is affiliated with the Visvesvaraya Technological University or VTU. It was founded by his holyness Dr. Sree Sree Sivakumara Swamiji. SIT Tumkur has been given its autonomous status by the University Grants Commission, New Delhi, Government of the state, and VTU, Belagavi. Approved by AICTE, it is one among the 130+ educational institutions run by the Sri Siddaganga Educational Society.

Dynamic Hack

Dynamic Hack is a first ever hackathon to be conducted at national level by Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur. It will be hosted by Team Dynamics (Project Oriented Community) under the supervision of Dr. N R Sunitha, Professor, Dept. of CSE.
