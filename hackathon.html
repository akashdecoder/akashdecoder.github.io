DYNAMIC HACK

EDUCATION IN PANDEMIC

5 June - 6 June, 2021

A 48 hours, National Level Virtual Hackathon

Dynamic Hack

Dynamic Hack is a first ever hackathon to be conducted at national level by Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering, Siddaganga Institute of Technology, Tumkur. It will be hosted by Team Dynamics (Project Oriented Community) under the supervision of Dr. N R Sunitha, Professor, Dept. of CSE.

This hackathon is based on the theme regarding the recent outbreak of CORONA VIRUS which has led to a global pandemic. It has affected many aspects of human life, ex: employment, education, shelter etc.

Education is one of the essential elements in building an individual's career. It must be imparted to every individual in the right way without any compromise, be it through offline mode or an online mode.

Though our world has already shown a great improvement in technological advancements to make the virtual mode as reliable as possible. We still urge to find better solutions to improve the ways by which education could be fruitful to individuals during these kinds of global pandemics.
